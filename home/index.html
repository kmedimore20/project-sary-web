Introducing the IoT system for
monitoring and managing farms

This project is developed by Karlo and is curently in the works. Please be patient, and stay tuned for news about the development.

Actions

Controll your farm on the go! No matter where you are, and which device you have, controlling your farm is only one step away.

Statistics

See the real time or historical data of your farm. Check the performance of systems and figure out solutions on the go.

Cloud storage

No need for local storage solutions. Use our cloud for storing all the data that you need. This data will always be available to you as long as you have an internet connection.

Secure

Security is our main focus. Data that is stored in our cloud and that is collected by our devices is only visible to you. You have full control of what data you save and what data you delete.

IoT devices

Check out our edge devices. There are a lot of prebuilt devices that can collect data and make actions. We also offer custom made devices, so you can have the best system for your farm.
